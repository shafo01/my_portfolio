About

KeepKreating was created as a portfolio and journal by Shafeeza. Shafeeza is an aspiring UI developer who has background in programming and UX. She has exposure to database design, back-end coding, front-end coding, REST APIs (JSON), User Experience, product development and e-learning. Her dream is to become an empathetic and creative UI engineer. She hopes to specialize in accessibility and performance optimization for web applications. Shafeeza also has an admiration for colours and the effect they have on our senses. As Shafeeza is learning she is documenting her journey.
